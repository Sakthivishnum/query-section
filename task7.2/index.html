Frequently Asked Question

Why is the moon sometimes during the day?

The moon is sometimes visible during the day due to its orbit around the Earth, which causes it to be in different positions relative to the Sun and our line of sight.

Why is the sky blue?

The sky appears blue due to Rayleigh scattering of sunlight in the Earth's atmosphere.
